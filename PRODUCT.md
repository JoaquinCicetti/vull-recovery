# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Active, athletic people in Argentina — gym-goers, runners, lifters, amateur
competitors — booking recovery sessions (massage, stretching, recovery work) at a
small recovery center. Recovery is part of their training, not a medical event.
They're motivated and performance-minded, usually booking fast from a phone
between training blocks. Spanish-speaking.

## Product Purpose
Let clients see plans/prices, find a real open slot, book it, and pay — fast —
with WhatsApp for confirmation. Success = a client books and pays in well under a
couple of minutes without having to message anyone, and the owner runs the day
from a simple admin. Built for zero fixed monthly cost.

## Brand Personality
Premium and performance-tech: disciplined, precise, confident. Energy comes from
sharpness and speed, not from loudness. Three words: focused, athletic,
dependable. It should feel like serious training equipment — not a clinic, not a
hype machine.

## Anti-references
- Sterile medical/clinic sites (cold white + teal, doctor stock photos).
- Generic SaaS dashboards (default Material/Bootstrap, identical card grids, the
  hero-metric template).
- Neon gym-bro aesthetics (aggressive neon-on-black, flames).
- Soft spa/wellness pastels (beige, lavender, overly calm).

## Design Principles
- **Speed is the feature.** Every screen makes the next action obvious and fast.
- **Earn trust through precision.** Tight alignment, real availability, honest
  status — competence over decoration.
- **Energy through contrast, not noise.** One confident green does the work.
- **Mobile-first.** Most clients book one-handed from a phone.
- **Quietly premium.** It should feel like high-end athletic gear.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Target WCAG AA contrast (dark text on the bright green; verify green on dark surfaces).
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`.
- Never rely on color alone for status — always pair with a label.
- Spanish UI.
